Investing Apps

Product design, Interaction Prototyping, Vibe Coding

Designing for friends, like-minded investors, and myself, I vibe coded a few apps:
• Daily Snapshot (AI summary of the market and indivdual stocks)
• P/E calculator (Justified PE modeling)
• DCF Upside Dashboard (Make Take The Edge Off Money - 5M)
• Property vs ETF Return Simulator (Make F*ck U Money - 20M)
This is an ongoing project aiming to build out a set of robust apps that help people visualize and eventually make more informed investment decisions based on their individual risk tolerance and investment goals.
